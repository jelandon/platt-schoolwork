Why should you learn about your car?

Savings

The more you know about your car, the more you'll save on maintenence and repairs. Once you learn enough, you can work on your own paying just for garage/tool rentals!

Safety

Nobody likes planning for the worst, but it's better than waiting around for the alternative. Let us help you gain the knowledge and self-confidence necessary to travel safely.

We love getting down and dirty, but don't worry if that's not you. We offer so many different classes, seminars, and workshops for all that there's never a dull moment.
