Neighborhood Camera Shop

Thinking of buying a new camera? The hardest choice is deciding where to shop. With so many options, from big-box retailers to online dealers and everything in between, where you buy is critical to the long-term happiness with your choice. Our expert knowledge combined with friendly service provide helpful and insightful guidance as you find the camera that is perfect for you.

Experienced Staff

We love photography! Every member of our staff will gladly talk about cameras and techniques as long as someone wants to hear it. We can also pair your camera with perfect accessories, everything from optics to professional studio equipment. Come in today and talk with us!

Try Before You Buy

Visit our showroom to test any camera before you buy! Customers can take both indoor and outdoor test shots, then review their photos on our computer. Whatever you prefer to shoot on, we have more options than any local retailer so you can compare the most features.

Free Maintenance

A camera can be a large investment and taking care of any mechanical parts and optics is critical. Every new camera purchased in our showroom comes with a free maintenance visit. If something ever goes wrong with an NCS camera, the owner can bring it in for at repair or replacement.
